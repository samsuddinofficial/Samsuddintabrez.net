Freelancer

Freelancing refers to a type of employment where individuals work independently on a project basis, offering their services to clients or businesses without being permanently employed by them. Freelancers are self-employed professionals who typically have expertise in a specific field or industry.

Data Entry

Data entry is a process of inputting and organizing various types of data into computer systems or databases. It involves accurately and efficiently capturing information from physical or digital sources and entering it into a software program or electronic format.

Web research

Web research refers to the process of gathering information and data from various online sources using internet search engines, databases, websites, and other online resources. It involves conducting searches, analyzing search results, visiting relevant websites, reading articles, reports, and other online content, and extracting relevant information for a specific purpose.

Leads Generation

Lead generation refers to the process of identifying and attracting potential customers or prospects who are interested in a product or service offered by a business. It involves gathering information about individuals or companies who have expressed interest or shown potential for becoming customers and then nurturing those leads to convert them into paying customers.

Classified Posting

Classified posting refers to the practice of placing advertisements or listings in online or print classified sections to promote products, services, or job opportunities. It involves creating concise and informative advertisements and posting them in relevant classified platforms to reach potential customers or applicants.
